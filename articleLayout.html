Article Layouts

Much of the fun of being creative is designing article layouts. Here you will find a sampling of the beginnings of various types of articles. They include articles about computer software, nutrition, practice building and decompression. Capturing the essense of the article and grabbing someones attention happens as quickly as a page turn so having an intriguing opening is always important.

Spread Articles

I don't often get the opportunity to create article spreads as more often than not, the pages run as an article/AD spread. I enjoy doing the spreads because it gives you a greater option at creating continuity and, to spread out visually. I generally have the articles laid out before I know what is going into the magzine so as they are generally rare, you will notice that even as spreads, they are sometimes pages that can be quickly adapted for single page usage.

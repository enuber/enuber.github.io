Company

HCMI, Inc.

Project Info

X-ray systems have come along way and, going digital was one of those big steps. Being able to get images immediately on a computer is a pretty big deal. I have limited experience with x-ray's myself outside of going to a dentist and, I remember them taking those, being almost done with cleaning before the images came out and then, they popped the small images in front of a light box so they could be seen. So, being digital was a big deal. I was given the text for the Ad as well as the picture of the unit. This particular Ad could have ended up very different than it did but, the end result I believe really made the purpose of the Ad stand out.

The Outcome

The tag line "Think Digital" I came up with after going thru stock images of x-ray shots. I likely should have been trying to fid a shot of a spine or back shot but, what HCMI was really pushing wasn't just another x-ray system but, the fact that it was digital. So, seeing a picture of the head, the idea of keeping this on your mind came to being. That think digital could have just as easily gone above the x-ray shot as there was plenty of space but, again for me it was an opportune suggestion to have digital litterally on the mind. I might have moved the top, line of the right side to the left but, I can see that the black side of the Ad stands out more because there isn't anything to distract from it.

I mentioned that the AD could have gone a completely different direction. The photo that I was supplied could have been the focal point and made much larger. It also shows a computer with an x-ray so could have sufficed on its own. I think it really comes down to the moment you are designing and what you see or interpret at a given moment.

Company

ProActive Health Systems

Project Info

It was important to the company that the Ad feature a four step process to Detox and Cleansing. It wasn't just about one product. Here one of the challenges was passing that information on while getting the product images that were apart of the protocol into the Ad.

The Outcome

In order to make this work in a quarter page area, I had to go thru alot of information on their products to try and summerize what was being offered in a single sentence. Each part of the protocols got a title and then that summerization. In order to break it apart and make those four steps stand out, they were given a thin border that surrounded the verbiage as well as went thru the specific product image to show that they were a group. The two products that were more imporatnt got top billing and; the same curved thin border used to group off each step was added around the whole ad to show that it was about the entire process.
